About Kerala Ticket Results — Who We Are

Your trusted daily companion for Kerala Lottery result updates, published since 2024.

About This Website

Kerala Ticket Results is an independent, informational web portal dedicated to publishing Kerala State Lottery draw results quickly and accurately every day. We are not affiliated with the Kerala Government or the Directorate of Kerala State Lotteries.

Our team monitors all official Kerala lottery draws — Karunya, Karunya Plus, Sthree Sakthi, Dhanalekshmi, Bhagyathara, Samrudhi, Suvarna Keralam, and Bumper lotteries — and publishes results within minutes of the 3:00 PM official announcement.

Our Commitment to Users

  • Results published daily by 3:15 PM after official draw completion at 3:00 PM IST.
  • All published data is cross-verified against official sources before publication.
  • Tamil and English language support for wider accessibility across South India.
  • Clean, ad-light interface optimised for mobile devices.
  • No misleading promotions, no false lucky number guarantees.

Full Legal Disclaimer

This website is an independent informational portal and is not affiliated with the Kerala State Lottery Department or the Government of Kerala.

We aggregate and publish results for the convenience of our users. While we strive for 100% accuracy, typographical errors or data discrepancies may occasionally occur.

Always verify your winning numbers with the official Kerala Government Gazette before making any prize claims or financial decisions.
