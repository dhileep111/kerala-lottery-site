How to Claim Kerala Lottery Prize — Complete Guide 2026

Step-by-step guide to claiming your Kerala lottery winnings — from ticket verification to receiving prize money.

Step-by-Step Prize Claim Process

  1. Verify your ticket: Cross-check your ticket number with the official result in the Kerala Government Gazette. Do NOT rely solely on third-party websites.
  2. Sign the ticket immediately: Sign on the back of the winning ticket to prevent misuse if lost or stolen.
  3. Gather required documents: Collect Aadhaar card, PAN card (mandatory for prizes above ₹10,000), and two passport-size photographs.
  4. Fill the prize claim form: Download or collect the form from the Lottery Department office. Fill all fields accurately.
  5. Visit the appropriate office: Go to the correct office based on your prize amount (agent / district office / directorate).
  6. Ticket verification: Officials verify authenticity under UV light and cross-check the serial number against official results.
  7. Tax deduction (TDS): TDS of 30% + applicable surcharge is deducted at source for prizes above ₹10,000.
  8. Prize payment: Prize money is paid via crossed cheque or direct bank transfer within the stipulated timeframe.

Required Documents Checklist

  • Original winning lottery ticket (signed on the back)
  • Aadhaar Card — original + photocopy
  • PAN Card — mandatory if prize exceeds ₹10,000
  • 2 recent passport-size photographs
  • Bank passbook copy or cancelled cheque (for bank transfer)
  • Duly filled prize claim form
